Ingredients
- 12 large red chillis
- 500g dark chocolate
- icing sugar
- black pepper

Directions
1. Remove the end, membrane and seeds from the chillis
2. Melt the chocolate in a bain marie
3. Pur half the chocolate in to the chillis and then dip / roll the chillis in the remaining chocolate
4. Freeze the chocolate covered chillis until the chocolate is hard
5. Put on a platter and generously dust with black pepper and icing sugar
